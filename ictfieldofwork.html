Flatline Agency


Who?


The company Flatinline Agency. Flatline Agency is a global disruptor in custom development & design experiences based in Amsterdam, New york and Bali. They deliver high-quality work - while leveraging the newest and best techniques available.


What roles are there?


They have many different roles inside the company. Such as:

  • ecommerce
  • Marketing & development
  • development
  • sites


The Culture inside Flatline Agency


The culture inside Flatinline Agency is very casual. People help eachother in a good working environment. The reason it is casual is because of the "game room". They have a game room for when you need to take a break. It is ofcourse business like, but the office makes you feel like home. Here is an example of a working environment:
You can find the image HERE.


What platform do they use?

They use all kind of platforms. they code and improve everything from Custom platforms, progressive web apps, apps, websites, SAAS, and AI / Deep learning applications in Python, NodeJS, WordPress, React (Native/Gatsby) & Flutter.


Would I see myself working here?

I would see myself working there, because they operate all around the world. This would mean that I can collaborate with diverse talent and make a significant impact in the digital world. Also working here means that i'll be part of a team that consistently delivers high-quality, innovative solutions that challenge and inspire.

Enappsys


Who?


"who are we?"
When we look in our crystal ball, we see transparent energy markets all across the globe. At EnAppSys, we are passionate about creating a level playing field for everyone working in the energy sector. For us, that means facilitating energy transition at the lowest possible cost.
source: Enappsys about page


What roles are there?

There are different roles in the company such as: Senior Energy Analyst, Senior Energy Consultant, product Consultant- power market guru and European sales Executive.


The Culture inside Flatline Agency

Inside the company they use the Down-to-earth philosophy. They "don't just shoot for the stars; we go beyond them." They also do a lot of activities with the employees. Such as brunches and after work activities.


What platform do they use?

Enappsys uses different kind of platforms. They use platforms such as: Python, calculation engines, aggregation engines, front end GUI and that all in AWS.


Would I see myself working here?

yes, I would see myself working here for many reasons. My father works at this company so I know what they do. I like the employees and the vibe around the office. I also think that it is very cool that they have many locations around the world. The multicultural part in the company is big and I really like it. I also think that the bosses are very nice and helpfull in many ways.

Utility Agency


Who?

Utility is an award-winning agency that crafts digital products that fuel tomorrow's brands. We partner with our clients to design and develop custom mobile applications and websites that scale. From Fortune 500 brands to innovative startups, we work with clients to bring their big ideas to life and transform their businesses.


What roles are there?

There are many jobs open such as: backend engineer, IOS engineer, brand designer, product designer, product manager and many more.


The Culture inside Utility

Although the company is very remote, they have offices in NYC. These offices are looking very cosy and fun. I think the people there love working and helping eachother. The office is made like it is your home, they did this so it makes you more comfertable to work in the office.


What platform do they use?

They use many platforms such as: JavaScript, Node.JS, react, PHP, SQL, bootstrap and many more.


Would I see myself working here?

I dont think I would see myself working there, the reason for that is because it is all remote or I have to move to LA or NYC. I Know I like working around people and having conversations. Also you cannot reach out super fast to one of your colleagues. I'm a social person so I dont think this company suits me the best.
